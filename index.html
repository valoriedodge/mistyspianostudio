About

Hi, I'm Misty

I studied elementary education with a minor in music in college. It's here that I found a LOVE of teaching children and more specifically teaching children music! I started teaching piano lessons when I was in high school and that gave me my direction for college. I now have a studio in my home in Fort Worth, TX where I am able to continue to teach and instill a love of music in my students.

I love teaching children and adults how to play the piano. I teach beginners through intermediate. Learning to play the piano is a wonderful skill that stimulates thinking and creativity. It requires dedication and is very rewarding. I would love to help you and your children gain this lifelong skill that will be enjoyable throughout your life.
